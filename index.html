Frequently Asked Questions...

How long does it take to set up the rig?

Our lighting rig takes a minimum of 30 minutes to set up pre-show. You can also request an early set up, where we set up a rig a day prior.

How do I order a custom lightshow?

Click on our "Book Now" button on the nav bar to make a custom purchase. We will review what you request, and work with you to make a show tailored to your vibe! We take 24 hours too respond and need a 2-4 buisness days to program a custom show.

What payment methods can I use?

We take Paypal, CashApp, Venmo, and cash!
